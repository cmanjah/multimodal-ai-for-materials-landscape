Multimodal AI for Materials Landscape Explorer

This page presents interactive data visualisations based on the paper Generative and multimodal AI for materials prediction and design: Progress, challenges, and perspectives by Liu, Anjah, Jolly et al. (IOP Publishing, Journal of Physics: Materials).

The analysis covers publications from 2020 to 2025 and highlights the rapid growth of multimodal and generative AI in materials research. Publication data were retrieved from the Elsevier Scopus database.

The data is last updated in March 2026. The reported numbers should be interpreted as indicators of overall trends rather than exact totals.
